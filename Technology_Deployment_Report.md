## ðŸ” Deployment Strategies

### Solar PV Deployment
- **Utility-scale Projects**: Large solar farms in Sindh and Punjab
- **Distributed Generation**: Rooftop solar installations in urban areas
- **Policy Support**: Feed-in tariffs and net metering

- **Coastal Projects**: Gharo-Keti Bandar corridor for large wind farms
- **Inland Projects**: Smaller wind farms in Balochistan
- **Regulatory Framework**: Streamlined permitting processes

### Hydropower Deployment
- **Run-of-River Projects**: Focus on rivers in northern areas
- **Storage Projects**: Limited sites for large dams
- **Environmental Considerations**: Careful site selection to minimize impact

- **Geothermal**: Explore potential in northern regions
- **Emerging Technologies**: Research and development for ocean energy
