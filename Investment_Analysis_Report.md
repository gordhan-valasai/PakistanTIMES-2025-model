- **Policy Uncertainty**: Changes in renewable energy policies

- **Phased Implementation**: Reduce early-stage risks

## üîÆ Conclusion

Pakistan's energy transition requires significant but manageable investment. The REN50 and REN60 scenarios offer the best balance between emissions reduction and investment requirements. A phased approach with strong public-private partnerships and international support will be crucial for success.
