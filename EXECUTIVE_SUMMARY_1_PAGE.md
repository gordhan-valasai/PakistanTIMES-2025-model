## üîç **Three Policy Insights**

### **1. Investment Efficiency**
- **REN30 ‚Üí REN70:** Additional $0.01B investment achieves **57.1% emissions reduction**
- **Cost per MtCO‚ÇÇ avoided:** ~$0.00002B (highly cost-effective)
- **Return on investment:** Significant in terms of climate benefits and energy security

### **2. Demand Growth Sensitivity**
- **High Growth (HEG):** Increases emissions by **~30%** across all renewable targets
- **Low Growth (LEG):** Reduces emissions by **~16%** vs Business-as-Usual
- **Policy implication:** Economic growth management is as important as renewable targets

### **3. Technology Deployment**
- **Solar & Wind:** Primary expansion drivers (realistic capacity factors)
- **Hydro:** Stable baseload (existing infrastructure advantage)
- **Storage:** Implicit in system planning (reserve margins)

## üéØ **Policy Recommendations**

### **Immediate (2025-2030)**
- **Set REN50 as minimum target** (28.6% emissions reduction)
- **Strengthen grid infrastructure** for renewable integration
- **Implement feed-in tariffs** for solar and wind projects

### **Medium-term (2030-2040)**
- **Aim for REN60-70 targets** (42.9-57.1% emissions reduction)
- **Deploy large-scale storage** solutions
- **Develop smart grid** capabilities

### **Long-term (2040-2050)**
- **Achieve 70% renewable share** across all demand scenarios
- **Export renewable energy** to regional markets
- **Establish Pakistan as** renewable energy leader in South Asia
