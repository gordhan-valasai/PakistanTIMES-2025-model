## üîß Technology Mix Analysis

### Solar PV Deployment
- **Share**: 50-60% of renewable capacity
- **Capacity Factor**: 18-22% (varies by region)
- **Cost Reduction**: 40-60% expected by 2030
- **Land Requirement**: 2-3 acres per MW

### Wind Power Deployment
- **Share**: 30-40% of renewable capacity
- **Capacity Factor**: 25-35% (coastal vs inland)
- **Cost Reduction**: 25-40% expected by 2030
- **Technical Potential**: 50+ GW identified

### Hydropower Deployment
- **Share**: 10-15% of renewable capacity
- **Capacity Factor**: 40-50% (seasonal variation)
- **Environmental Considerations**: Careful site selection needed
- **Pumped Storage**: Important for grid balancing

### Other Technologies
- **Biomass**: 2-5% contribution, waste-to-energy potential
- **Geothermal**: Limited potential, exploration ongoing
- **Ocean Energy**: Long-term potential, early R&D stage

### Solar Potential
- **Sindh**: Highest solar irradiation, ideal for large-scale projects
- **Punjab**: Good potential, population centers enable distributed generation
- **Balochistan**: Excellent potential, transmission challenges
- **KPK**: Moderate potential, mountainous terrain

### Wind Potential
- **Coastal Sindh**: Excellent wind resources, Gharo-Keti Bandar corridor
- **Balochistan**: Good potential, infrastructure challenges
- **Northern Areas**: Limited potential, terrain constraints

### Hydropower Potential
- **Northern Mountains**: Major hydropower potential, environmental sensitivity
- **River Systems**: Run-of-river projects, seasonal variation
- **Storage Projects**: Limited sites, long development timelines

## ‚ö° System Integration Challenges

### Grid Stability
- **Intermittency**: Solar and wind variability
- **Forecasting**: Improved weather prediction needed
- **Flexibility**: Require flexible generation and demand response

### Storage Requirements
- **Short-term**: Battery storage for daily cycling (4-8 hours)
- **Long-term**: Pumped hydro for seasonal storage
- **Emerging**: Hydrogen storage for multi-day balancing

### Transmission Needs
- **New Lines**: Connect renewable-rich areas to load centers
- **Grid Upgrades**: Smart grid technologies for better control
- **Cross-border**: Potential for regional electricity trade
