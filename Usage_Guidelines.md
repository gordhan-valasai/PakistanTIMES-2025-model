## üìà Data Interpretation

### Understanding Scenarios
- **Renewable Targets**: REN30, REN50, REN60, REN70 represent different levels of ambition
- **Demand Scenarios**: BAU, HEG, LEG, MEG represent different economic growth pathways
- **Combination**: Each scenario combines a renewable target with a demand scenario

### Key Metrics
- **Renewable Share**: Percentage of electricity from renewable sources
- **Emissions**: CO2 emissions from electricity generation
- **Investment**: Capital requirements for energy infrastructure
- **Economic Impact**: Broader economic implications
