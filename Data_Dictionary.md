## ðŸ“ˆ Key Parameters

### Renewable Energy Targets
- **REN30**: 30% renewable energy share by 2050
- **REN50**: 50% renewable energy share by 2050
- **REN60**: 60% renewable energy share by 2050
- **REN70**: 70% renewable energy share by 2050

### Demand Scenarios
- **BAU**: Business-as-Usual demand growth
- **HEG**: High Energy Growth scenario
- **LEG**: Low Energy Growth scenario
- **MEG**: Medium Energy Growth scenario

## ðŸ“Š Data Fields

### Scenario Summary
- **Scenario**: Combination of renewable target and demand scenario
- **Renewable_Target_%**: Target renewable energy share
- **Demand_Scenario**: Demand growth scenario
- **Final_Renewable_Share_2050_%**: Achieved renewable share in 2050
- **Avg_Renewable_Share_%**: Average renewable share over the period
- **Cumulative_Emissions_2014_2050_GtCO2**: Total emissions from 2014 to 2050
- **Annual_Emissions_2050_MtCO2**: Emissions in the year 2050
- **Total_Investment_2014_2050_Billion_USD**: Total investment required
- **Final_Demand_2050_TWh**: Electricity demand in 2050
- **Emissions_Reduction_vs_BAU_%**: Reduction compared to BAU scenario

### Detailed Annual Results
- **Year**: Calendar year
- **Scenario**: Scenario identifier
- **Electricity_Demand_TWh**: Total electricity demand
- **Renewable_Share_%**: Share of renewable energy
- **Annual_Emissions_MtCO2**: Annual CO2 emissions
- **Cumulative_Investment_Billion_USD**: Cumulative investment to date
- **Annual_Investment_Billion_USD**: Investment in the current year
- **Capacity_Additions_GW**: New capacity added
- **System_Cost_USD_MWh**: Levelized cost of energy

### Renewable Trajectories
- **Year**: Calendar year
- **Scenario**: Scenario identifier
- **Solar_Capacity_GW**: Installed solar capacity
- **Wind_Capacity_GW**: Installed wind capacity
- **Hydro_Capacity_GW**: Installed hydro capacity
- **Other_Renewable_Capacity_GW**: Other renewable capacity
- **Total_Renewable_Capacity_GW**: Total renewable capacity
- **Renewable_Generation_TWh**: Renewable electricity generation
- **Renewable_Share_%**: Share of renewable energy

### Investment Analysis
- **Scenario**: Scenario identifier
- **Total_Investment_Billion_USD**: Total investment required
- **Solar_Investment_Billion_USD**: Investment in solar energy
- **Wind_Investment_Billion_USD**: Investment in wind energy
- **Hydro_Investment_Billion_USD**: Investment in hydro energy
- **Grid_Investment_Billion_USD**: Investment in grid infrastructure
- **Storage_Investment_Billion_USD**: Investment in energy storage
- **Annual_Avg_Investment_Billion_USD**: Average annual investment
- **Peak_Annual_Investment_Billion_USD**: Peak annual investment
- **Investment_per_TWh_Demand**: Investment intensity

### Emissions Analysis
- **Scenario**: Scenario identifier
- **Cumulative_Emissions_GtCO2**: Total emissions 2014-2050
- **Annual_Emissions_2050_MtCO2**: Emissions in 2050
- **Emissions_Reduction_vs_BAU_%**: Reduction compared to BAU
- **Emissions_Intensity_gCO2_kWh**: CO2 intensity of electricity
- **Peak_Emissions_Year**: Year of peak emissions
- **Peak_Emissions_MtCO2**: Value of peak emissions
- **Annual_Reduction_Rate_%**: Average annual reduction rate
- **Carbon_Budget_Alignment_%**: Alignment with carbon budget

### Technology Deployment
- **Scenario**: Scenario identifier
- **Technology**: Energy technology
- **Capacity_2014_GW**: Installed capacity in 2014
- **Capacity_2050_GW**: Installed capacity in 2050
- **Capacity_Growth_%**: Percentage growth
- **Share_of_Total_%**: Share of total capacity
- **Investment_Required_Billion_USD**: Required investment
- **Capacity_Factor_%**: Average capacity factor
- **LCOE_USD_MWh**: Levelized cost of energy

### Economic Impact
- **Scenario**: Scenario identifier
- **GDP_Growth_%**: Impact on GDP growth
- **Job_Creation_Million**: Jobs created
- **Energy_Cost_Savings_Billion_USD**: Savings on energy costs
- **Import_Reduction_Billion_USD**: Reduction in energy imports
- **Health_Benefits_Billion_USD**: Value of health benefits
- **Total_Economic_Benefit_Billion_USD**: Total economic benefit
- **Benefit_Cost_Ratio**: Ratio of benefits to costs
